Ease: Virtual Closet App Design

Click here to view the full report in a new tab.

Team: Thy Dao and Susie Nguyen

Date: August — December 2019

Summary: Ease is a virtual closet organizer app that aims to ease people’s hectic lives by saving time in organizing their clothes, planning their outfits, and overall eliminating worries from their daily lives. The app is designed by Thy Dao and Susie Nguyen, who both played in equal role in all steps of the iterative design process from ideation to evaluation.
